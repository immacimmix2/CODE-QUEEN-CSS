I’m Ainomugisha Immaculate, a passionate STEM educator and advocate for youth skilling in Uganda.With over 7 years of hands-on experience in electronics, robotics, and technical training.
I specialize in equipping young minds with the skills they need to thrive in today’s technology-driven world.

I’ve trained and mentored over 100 university students and out-of-school youth through immersive programs that combine practical engineering, soft skills, and innovation. I currently lead internship programs that bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world application, nurturing future engineers, entrepreneurs, and changemakers.

My vision is to transform technical education through hands-on learning, collaborative industry partnerships, and policy influence—ensuring that no learner is left behind in the digital age.
When I’m not building circuits or mentoring students, I enjoy exploring ways technology can impact agriculture, healthcare, and everyday life across Africa.
